About the Project

A Pigeon's Quest for Recognition

On the NYUAD campus, the cats are celebrities. Students stop to pet them and shower them with affection. But the pigeons? Nobody spares them a second glance. They are often ignored and treated as pests. The Pigeon Who Tried follows determined pigeons who decide enough is enough — it's time to restore the balance and win the attention they deserve.

This interactive web comic features a branching narrative. At a pivotal moment, you decide to restore the justice for the pigeons: will they take a serious action, or try to make peace with the cats? Each path leads to a different ending with unique scenes and sound effects.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the comic features page-flip animations, preloaded image caching for instant transitions, and a carefully crafted sound design that reacts to key story moments.
